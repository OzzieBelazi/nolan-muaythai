Professional Muay Thai Near Crystal Palace

Crystal Palace has plenty of gyms — but if you want to learn real Muay Thai from a real champion, Nolan Muay Thai is 10 minutes away in Beckenham. No generic fitness boxing. No cardio kickboxing. Just authentic Muay Thai taught by people who've fought at the highest level.


Head coach Liam Nolan is a WBC Muay Thai World Champion, IKF British Champion, and ONE Championship veteran. He's coached fighters to national titles and works with complete beginners every week. Whether you're looking for fitness, self-defence, or a competitive path — this is where serious training happens in South London.


We're an easy journey from Crystal Palace, Sydenham, Anerley and Upper Norwood — and several of our members come from the SE19/SE26 area.

What We Offer

Training for Every Goal

  • Beginner Classes — No experience needed. Learn fundamentals in a supportive environment
  • Advanced / Fighter Prep — Sparring, advanced technique, fight camps
  • Junior Muay Thai — Sunday 11AM–12PM for kids. DBS-checked coaches. Great for discipline and confidence
  • Private 1-2-1 Training — Focused sessions with Liam tailored to your goals
