## 2. Liam Nolan - Key Bio (for /coaches page)

### Personal Details
- **Full name:** Liam Emmanuel Nolan-Guigne
- **Born:** 27 May 1997, Bordeaux, France (raised in North London)
- **Nickname:** "Lethal"
- **Height:** 6'1.5" (187cm)
- **Weight:** 77kg / 170lb
- **Stance:** Southpaw
- **Style:** Muay Thai
- **Fighting out of:** London, England
- **Years active:** 2013â€“2025 (retired)
- **Record:** 22W (15 KOs) - 9L - 1D

### Background Story
- Grew up in North London, raised by single mother in financially strained household
- Tried Wing Chun and Taekwondo before discovering Muay Thai at age 13 (2010)
- Trained at The Minotaur Gym / Knowlesy Academy under **Christian Knowles** (key mentor/father figure)
- Left school at 15-16 to pursue professional Muay Thai career
- Christian Knowles provided technical guidance, personal support, transportation, and employment at the gym

### Qualifications (from nolanmuaythai.com)
- Fully qualified Level 2 Personal Trainer
- Level 3 Personal Trainer
- Fully DBS checked
- "Has coached some of the UK's finest up and coming fighters"

### Titles & Achievements
- **WBC Muay Thai World Middleweight Champion** (2021) - beat Youssef Boughanem
- **WMC Intercontinental -72.5kg Champion** (2017)
- **IKF Pro Muay Thai British Champion** (2017)
- **Roar Combat League World Super Welterweight Champion** (2018)
- **IFMA World Championships -75kg** (2022)
- **Thai Fighter UK Fighter of the Year** (2018, 2021)
- ONE Championship contract (2019-2025), ONE record: 5-7
- ONE Performance of the Night award (vs Kim Kyung Lock)

### Coaching Credentials
- Runs **Nolan Muay Thai** gym (Beckenham area)
- Guest tutor on **Liam Harrison Training** platform (Southpaw Muay Thai online course)
  - Described by Liam Harrison as "among the best in the world"
  - Course covers: signature southpaw combinations, footwork & angles, counter-striking, clinch mastery
- **Patreon:** https://www.patreon.com/LiamNolanTraining - "Technique, Drills, and Conditioning videos" (70 posts)
- **Beet It Sport Ambassador** (since 2023)

### Key Coach: Christian Knowles
