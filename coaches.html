Liam Nolan

Head Muay Thai Coach & Founder

Starting Muay Thai at the age of 13 Liam has been training and competing for over 14 years at the highest level and Winning multiple World Championships. A recognised name on the global Muay Thai stage and signed for the largest Muay Thai sports organisation, One Championship, Liam has now turned to coaching taking on beginners to seasoned pros.


Liam is the current reigning WBC Middleweight World Champion as well as holding belts in the IKF, WMc and Roar Combat League. Liam is a Fully qualified Level two personal trainer & Level three personal trainer and is fully DBS checked.


Liam Nolan has coached some of the UK's finest up and coming fighters and is eager to share his knowledge with you!
