4. Assumption of Risk

I acknowledge that participation in a Muay Thai competition involves serious and inherent risks of physical injury including but not limited to:

  1. Bruises, lacerations, sprains, fractures, dislocations, and dental injuries.
  2. Concussion, traumatic brain injury, and other head/neck trauma.
  3. Internal injuries, joint damage, and ligament/tendon tears.
  4. Cardiovascular events including cardiac arrest.
  5. In rare cases, permanent disability or death.

I understand these risks cannot be eliminated regardless of the care taken by the event organisers, officials, or medical staff present. I voluntarily assume full responsibility for all risks of injury, illness, or loss arising from my participation.
