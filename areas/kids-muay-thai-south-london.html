South London's Premier Kids Muay Thai Programme

Finding quality children's martial arts in South London can be hit-and-miss. Most gyms bolt on a kids' class as an afterthought. At Nolan Muay Thai, our junior programme is taught with the same seriousness and care as our adult classes — because we believe children deserve proper coaching too.


Based at Beckenham Boxing Club, we're centrally located in South East London with easy access from Bromley, Penge, Crystal Palace, West Wickham, Orpington, Lewisham, Sydenham and surrounding areas. New Beckenham station is 2 minutes from our door.


Our gym is led by Liam Nolan — a WBC Muay Thai World Champion, ONE Championship veteran, and fully qualified Level 2 & 3 Personal Trainer. Every coach working with children is fully DBS checked.


Why Muay Thai for Kids?

Muay Thai is one of the best martial arts for children. It develops the whole body — coordination, cardiovascular fitness, strength, flexibility — while teaching discipline, respect and focus. Children who train regularly tend to perform better at school, show improved behaviour at home, and develop a quiet confidence that stays with them into adulthood.
