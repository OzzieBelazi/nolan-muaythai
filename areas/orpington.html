The Best Muay Thai Training Near Orpington

If you're in Orpington and searching for Muay Thai or kickboxing classes, Nolan Muay Thai is well worth the short trip. Based at Beckenham Boxing Club, we're around 15 minutes from Orpington by car — and there's nothing like us closer.


Most gyms in the Orpington area offer generic fitness classes or mixed martial arts without specialist focus. At Nolan Muay Thai, Muay Thai is all we do. Our head coach Liam Nolan is a former WBC Muay Thai World Champion and ONE Championship fighter with over 30 professional bouts. He's now dedicated to coaching and developing the next generation of fighters — and helping everyday people get fit through authentic Muay Thai training.


We have members travelling from Orpington, St Mary Cray, Petts Wood and Chislehurst. The quality of instruction makes the drive worth it — you'll learn real Muay Thai, not watered-down cardio kickboxing.

Training Options

Classes for Everyone

  • Beginner Muay Thai — Zero experience required. We'll teach you stance, movement, all 8 weapons (fists, elbows, knees, shins)
  • Intermediate & Advanced — Technical drilling, sparring, clinch work, fight strategy
  • Junior Muay Thai — Sunday mornings 11AM–12PM for kids. Builds discipline, coordination and confidence. All coaches fully DBS checked
  • Private Sessions — 1-2-1 or small group training with Liam, tailored to your specific goals

Getting Here from Orpington

  • 🚗 By car: 15 minutes via the A232 through Bromley, or A21/A222. Free street parking on High Street
  • 🚂 By train: Orpington to Bromley South (6 mins), then Bromley South to New Beckenham (8 mins). The gym is a 2-minute walk from the station
  • 🚌 By bus: 61 to Bromley, then 227 or 354 to Beckenham
