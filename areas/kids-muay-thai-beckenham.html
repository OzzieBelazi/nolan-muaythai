Beckenham's Best Kids Martial Arts Programme

Nolan Muay Thai runs junior Muay Thai classes in Beckenham every Sunday morning. Based right on the High Street at Beckenham Boxing Club, we're the only specialist Muay Thai gym in Beckenham — and our kids' programme is something we're genuinely proud of.


Our gym is led by Liam Nolan, a WBC Muay Thai World Champion and ONE Championship veteran. The junior programme isn't an afterthought — it's a core part of what we do. Children learn real Muay Thai technique in a structured, age-appropriate environment that prioritises safety, fun and personal development.


What Your Child Will Learn

  • Muay Thai fundamentals — Proper stance, basic strikes (punches, kicks), movement and defence
  • Discipline & focus — Following instructions, respecting coaches and training partners
  • Physical fitness — Cardiovascular health, coordination, balance, strength and flexibility
  • Confidence & self-esteem — Mastering new skills gives children genuine belief in themselves
  • Anti-bullying awareness — Understanding boundaries and when/how to walk away

Parent FAQs

Is it safe?
Absolutely. All coaches are fully DBS checked. There is no full-contact sparring in junior classes. Sessions are supervised at all times.


My child has never done martial arts — is that OK?
Perfect, actually. Most of our juniors started with zero experience. We teach everything from scratch.


Will it make my child aggressive?
The opposite. Martial arts teach discipline, respect and emotional control. Children who train in Muay Thai tend to be calmer and more focused, not more aggressive.


Can I watch?
Yes — parents are welcome to stay and watch during sessions.
