## Welcome to Nolan Muay Thai

#### Liam Nolan - Head Muay Thai Coach
Starting Muay Thai at the age of 13 Liam has been training and competing for over 14 years at the highest level and Winning multiple World Championships. A recognised name on the global Muay Thai stage and signed for the largest Muay Thai sports organisation, One Championship, Liam has now turned to coaching taking on beginners to seasoned pros. Liam is the current reigning WBC Middleweight World Champion as well as holding belts in the IKF, WMc and Roar Combat League. Liam is a Fully qualified Level two personal trainer & Level three personal trainer and is fully DBS checked. Liam Nolan has coached some of the UK's finest up and coming fighters and is eager to share his knowledge with you !

### Liam is also available for private 1-2-1 sessions or small groups working on the following:

- Technique
- General Fitness
- Pad Work
- Drills

All session are tailored to meet your specific goals and targets within a bespoke training structure.

## Nolan Muay Thai - Our Values & Mission

#### Train with Professional Coaches
Our Muay Thai Boxing gym offers training with professional fighters who have competed at the highest levels. They have the knowledge to help you improve your technique and reach your goals.

#### Fitness and Conditioning
Muay Thai Boxing is an energetic and exciting combat sport that helps build discipline and wellbeing. Our core mission at Nolan Muay Thai is the promotion of Muay Thai to promote wellbeing, Fitness, Health and social skills for our youth.

#### Beginners Welcome
Never tried Muay Thai Boxing before? No problem! Our classes are designed for beginners and experienced fighters alike. Our trainers will work with you to help you learn and improve, no matter what your skill level.

#### Flexible Schedule
We understand that everyone has a busy schedule, so we offer a variety of class times to choose from.

#### Safe and Supportive Environment
At Nolan Muay Thai, we pride ourselves on creating a safe and supportive environment for all of our students. Our trainers will work with you to help you achieve your goals while also ensuring that you train safely and avoid injury.

#### Compete at the Highest Levels
If you're interested in competing, we can help you get there. Our trainers have experience competing at the highest levels and can help you prepare for amateur and professional fights.
