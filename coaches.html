Liam Nolan

Head Muay Thai Coach & Founder

Starting Muay Thai at the age of 13, Liam has been training and competing for over 14 years at the highest level, winning multiple World Championships. Signed for ONE Championship — the largest Muay Thai sports organisation — Liam has now turned to coaching, taking on beginners to seasoned pros.


Liam is the reigning WBC Middleweight World Champion as well as holding belts in the IKF, WMC and Roar Combat League. He is a fully qualified Level 2 & Level 3 Personal Trainer and is fully DBS checked.

Andre

Coach & Active Fighter

With over 8 years across Muay Thai, MMA, Taekwondo and Jiu Jitsu, Andre brings a well-rounded martial arts perspective to his coaching. A decorated amateur competitor — gold at the WKU Amateur World Championships and silver at the WKO Amateur Worlds — alongside multiple golds in Jiu Jitsu and medals in Taekwondo.


An active fighter with international experience including three bouts in Thailand, a fight on Max Muay Thai, and a victory at the O2 Arena on the Hitman Fight League card. Still competing and driven to achieve more.

Matt Doran

Junior Muay Thai Coach

Matt is a Muay Thai coach with amateur fight experience, having trained at renowned gyms including Double K and Semtex. He has coached juniors and adults, and worked ringside as a Muay Thai pundit at London's O2.


Alongside combat sports, Matt is a calisthenics coach specialising in bodyweight strength and hand balancing. With a background as a PE teacher, PT tutor, and coach to athletes of all ages, he brings both technical knowledge and teaching experience to every session.
