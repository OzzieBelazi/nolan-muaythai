Children's Muay Thai — Near Bromley, Safe & Fun

Looking for kids' martial arts classes near Bromley? Nolan Muay Thai runs dedicated Junior Muay Thai sessions every Sunday morning, just 10 minutes from Bromley town centre.


This isn't a bolt-on kids' class at a fitness gym. We're a specialist Muay Thai gym led by Liam Nolan — a WBC World Champion and ONE Championship fighter. Our junior programme is designed specifically for children, focusing on:

  • 🥊 Discipline & Respect — Structured classes with clear expectations
  • 💪 Fitness & Coordination — Full-body exercise disguised as fun
  • 🧠 Confidence — Learning a real skill builds genuine self-belief
  • 🤝 Social Skills — Training with other kids in a supportive environment
  • 🛡️ Self-Defence Awareness — Understanding boundaries and personal safety

All coaches are fully DBS checked. Safety is our absolute priority.

Class Details & Getting Here

Junior Muay Thai Sessions

  • 📅 When: Every Sunday, 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • 📍 Where: Beckenham Boxing Club, 248 High Street, BR3 1DZ
  • 👶 Ages: Contact us for age ranges and availability
  • 💰 Pricing: Call for kids' class prices
  • 👕 What to wear: Comfortable sportswear, trainers. No special equipment needed to start
  • ✅ Safety: All coaches fully DBS checked, first aid trained

Your child doesn't need any experience — we start from scratch and progress at their pace. Many of our junior members had never tried any martial art before joining.

Getting Here from Bromley

  • 🚂 By train: Bromley South to New Beckenham (8 mins). The gym is a 2-minute walk from the station
