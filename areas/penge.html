Penge's Closest Muay Thai Gym

Living in Penge and want to train Muay Thai? You're practically on our doorstep. Nolan Muay Thai at Beckenham Boxing Club is just 5 minutes from Penge — walkable in 15 minutes, or one stop on the train from Penge East to New Beckenham.


We're a specialist Muay Thai and kickboxing gym, not a generic fitness centre. Our head coach Liam Nolan is a WBC World Champion who fought on the biggest stages in the sport including ONE Championship. He now coaches full-time, working with everyone from complete beginners to professional fighters.


Penge residents make up a good chunk of our membership — it's the easiest commute to world-class Muay Thai coaching you'll find in South East London.
