West Wickham's Nearest Professional Muay Thai Gym

There's no dedicated Muay Thai gym in West Wickham — but there's a world-class one just down the road. Nolan Muay Thai at Beckenham Boxing Club is under 10 minutes from West Wickham by car, making it the closest professional Muay Thai and kickboxing gym for West Wickham residents.


Our head coach Liam Nolan is a WBC Muay Thai World Champion who competed for ONE Championship. With a 22-9-1 professional record and 15 knockouts, Liam brings elite-level knowledge to every class — whether you're throwing your first punch or preparing for a fight.


We already have several members who travel from West Wickham and the surrounding Hayes and Shortlands areas. The short journey is worth it for coaching you won't find at a regular fitness gym.

What We Offer

Classes for All Ages & Levels

  • Beginner Muay Thai — No experience needed. Learn strikes, clinch, footwork and defence
  • Advanced / Fighter Training — Sparring, fight camps, competitive preparation
  • Kids / Junior Muay Thai — Sundays 11AM–12PM. Builds discipline, confidence and fitness. All coaches DBS checked
  • Private 1-2-1 Sessions — Personal training with Liam, tailored to your goals

Getting Here from West Wickham

  • 🚗 By car: 8-10 minutes via the A222 or Beckenham Road. Street parking available
  • 🚌 By bus: 194 towards Beckenham, then a short walk. Or 354 direct
  • 🚂 By train: West Wickham to New Beckenham — change at Elmers End or Clock House. The gym is 2 minutes from New Beckenham station
