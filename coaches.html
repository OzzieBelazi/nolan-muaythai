Liam Nolan

Head Muay Thai Coach & Founder

A multiple World Champion and ONE Championship veteran, Liam has competed at the highest level for over 14 years. Now focused on coaching, he brings that elite fight experience to every session — training beginners through to seasoned pros with the same intensity and attention to detail.


Liam has coached some of the UK's finest up-and-coming fighters and is passionate about developing talent at every level. Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or preparing for a fight, Liam builds your game from the ground up.

Andre

Coach & Active Fighter

With over 8 years across Muay Thai, MMA, Taekwondo and Jiu Jitsu, Andre brings a well-rounded martial arts perspective to his coaching. His multi-discipline background means he can develop fighters across all aspects of striking, grappling and movement.


An active competitor with international fight experience — including bouts in Thailand and a victory at the O2 Arena — Andre knows what it takes to prepare, perform and improve. He coaches with the mindset of someone still in the game, pushing his students the same way he pushes himself.

Matt Doran

Junior Muay Thai Coach

Matt is a Muay Thai coach with amateur fight experience, having trained at renowned gyms including Double K and Semtex. He has coached juniors and adults, and worked ringside as a Muay Thai pundit at London's O2.


Alongside combat sports, Matt is a calisthenics coach specialising in bodyweight strength and hand balancing. With a background as a PE teacher, PT tutor, and coach to athletes of all ages, he brings both technical knowledge and teaching experience to every session.

  • 🤸 Calisthenics & Movement Specialist
