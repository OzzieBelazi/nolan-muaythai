Kids Thai Boxing Coach — Matt Doran

Matt is a Muay Thai coach with amateur fight experience, having trained at renowned gyms including Double K and Semtex. He has coached juniors and adults, and worked ringside as a Muay Thai pundit at London's O2.


Alongside combat sports, Matt is a calisthenics coach, specialising in bodyweight strength and hand balancing to improve movement, control, and athletic performance. With a background as a PE teacher, PT tutor, and coach to athletes of all ages, he brings both technical knowledge and teaching experience to every session.

Qualifications

  • 🏋️ Active IQ Level 3 Personal Trainer
