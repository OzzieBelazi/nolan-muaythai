Bromley's Closest Professional Muay Thai Gym

If you're looking for Muay Thai classes near Bromley, Nolan Muay Thai is your best option. Based at Beckenham Boxing Club on the High Street, we're just a short journey from Bromley town centre — around 10 minutes by car or a quick bus ride on the 227 or 354.


Unlike generic fitness gyms, we specialise exclusively in Muay Thai and kickboxing. Our head coach Liam Nolan is a WBC Muay Thai World Champion and ONE Championship veteran with a 22-9-1 professional record. You won't find this level of coaching anywhere else in the Bromley area.


Whether you're a complete beginner looking to get fit, or an experienced fighter wanting to sharpen your skills, we have classes to suit every level. Many of our current members travel from Bromley, Bromley South, and Bromley Common because the quality of coaching simply isn't available closer to home.

What We Offer

Classes for Every Level

  • Beginner Muay Thai — Learn the fundamentals: stance, footwork, punches, kicks, knees and elbows
  • Advanced Muay Thai — Sharpen technique, sparring, fight preparation
  • Kids / Junior Muay Thai — Sundays 11AM–12PM, fully DBS-checked coaches
  • Private 1-2-1 Sessions — Tailored training with Liam Nolan
  • Fitness & Conditioning — Muay Thai-based fitness for anyone

Getting Here from Bromley

  • 🚗 By car: 10 minutes via the A222. Free street parking on High Street
  • 🚌 By bus: 227 or 354 from Bromley town centre, alight at New Beckenham
  • 🚂 By train: New Beckenham station is a 2-minute walk from the gym. Direct trains from Bromley South (8 mins)
